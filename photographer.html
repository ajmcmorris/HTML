Lana Miller

Photographer

Currently, I can be found dodging taxis on my bicycle in my new hometown, New York City, exploring new cultures in far off lands, or chowing down on delicious food while sipping on an exquisite craft cocktail.

Hailing from the waterlogged city of Seattle, it's where I learned how to dodge raindrops while clicking a shutter. I then enjoyed a short stint in the beatiful city of Paris where i realized I wasn't quite French enough to make a living there. Post France, I journeyed to Los angeles to dry out the long soggy years of my Seattle days.
